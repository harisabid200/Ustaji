# UstaJi — App Guide for Judges

> This document walks you through the UstaJi app step-by-step — what each screen does, how a real user would use it, and the two complete journeys: one for a **customer** looking for help, and one for a **service provider** looking for work.

---

## What is UstaJi?

UstaJi (meaning "Master" or "Expert" in Urdu) is a mobile app that connects people in Pakistan who need home services — like fixing an AC, calling a plumber, or hiring a tutor — with skilled, verified workers nearby.

Instead of calling friends for phone numbers or haggling over prices, you simply **chat with UstaJi in your own language** (English, Urdu, or Roman Urdu). The app understands what you need, finds the best available person, gives you a fair price upfront, schedules the appointment, and lets you track the whole job from start to finish.

---

## Two Sides of the App

UstaJi serves **two types of users** with completely different experiences:

| 🏠 **Customer** | 🔧 **Service Provider** |
|---|---|
| Someone who needs a service done at their home | A skilled worker (plumber, electrician, AC tech, etc.) |
| Chats with AI to describe their problem | Receives job offers and chooses which ones to accept |
| Gets matched with the best available provider | Manages their daily schedule and earnings |
| Tracks the booking from start to finish | Updates job status and reports delays |

When you open the app for the first time, you choose which side you are on.

## The Customer Journey

### Step 1: Sign Up & Choose Your Role

When you open UstaJi for the first time, you see the **Welcome Screen** with the UstaJi logo and the tagline *"Pakistan's smartest service marketplace."*

You enter:
- Your **name**
- Your **email**
- A **password**
- And most importantly — you pick: **"I need services"** (customer) or **"I provide services"** (provider)

If you already have an account, you can switch to the **Login** mode and sign in.

---

### Step 2: Home Screen — Your Dashboard

After signing in as a customer, you land on the **Home Screen**. It greets you with a time-appropriate Urdu greeting:
- Morning → *"Subah Bakhair 🌅"*
- Afternoon → *"Salam 👋"*
- Evening → *"Shab Bakhair 🌙"*

On this screen you see:

**Service Categories** — 12 colorful cards arranged in a grid:
- ❄️ AC Repair
- 🔧 Plumbing
- ⚡ Electrical
- 🧹 Cleaning
- 🪵 Carpentry
- 📚 Tutoring
- 💄 Beauty
- 🚗 Mechanic
- 🎨 Painting
- 🚐 Driver
- 📺 Appliances
- 🌬️ AC Installation

Each card shows the service name in **both English and Urdu**.

**Recent Bookings** — If you have past bookings, they appear here with their current status (Pending, Confirmed, In Progress, Completed).

Tapping any category card takes you to a screen showing **available providers** in that category — their names, ratings, reviews, and prices.

---

### Step 3: Chat with UstaJi — The Heart of the App

This is where the magic happens. Tap the **💬 Chat** tab at the bottom.

UstaJi greets you in Roman Urdu:

> *"Assalam o Alaikum! 👋 Main UstaJi hoon — aapka agentic service assistant. Koi bhi service chahiye — plumber, electrician, AC technician, tutor — bas batayein!"*

You can type your request in **any language you're comfortable with**:
- English: *"My AC is not cooling, I need someone in G-11 tomorrow morning"*
- Roman Urdu: *"AC kharab ho gaya hai, kal subah G-11 mein koi chahiye"*
- Urdu script also works

**What happens next (the conversation flow):**

1. **UstaJi understands your request** — It figures out:
   - What service you need (AC repair)
   - Where you are (G-11)
   - When you need it (tomorrow morning)
   - How urgent it is

2. **It finds the best provider** — Behind the scenes, it scores every available worker based on:
   - How close they are to your location
   - Their rating and reviews
   - Whether they are available at your requested time
   - Their price
   - Their on-time track record

3. **It shows you a price** — You get a clear breakdown:
   - Base service charge
   - Complexity factor (simple vs difficult job)
   - Urgency surcharge (if any)
   - Travel cost
   - **Total price in PKR**

4. **It proposes a time** — If the best provider is free at your requested time, great. If not, UstaJi suggests **alternative time slots** that appear as tappable chips at the bottom of the chat.

5. **You confirm the booking** — Quick reply buttons appear: *"✅ Book karo"*, *"🔄 Doosra provider"*, or *"❌ Cancel"*

Throughout this conversation, you can see a small **stage indicator** at the top (like "Request Understood", "Providers Found", "Pricing Ready") so you always know where you are in the process.

If the AI is thinking, you see a **typing animation** — just like WhatsApp.

---

### Step 4: Track Your Booking

Once you confirm, the booking appears in your **📋 Bookings** tab. Each booking shows:
- The service type
- Your provider's name
- Current status with color coding:
  - 🟡 **Pending** — Waiting for provider to confirm
  - 🔵 **Confirmed** — Provider accepted, job scheduled
  - 🟣 **En Route** — Provider is on the way
  - 🟠 **In Progress** — Work is being done
  - 🟢 **Completed** — Job finished

Tap any booking to see the **full details page** with:
- Provider name and contact
- Exact scheduled time
- Price breakdown
- A live status timeline showing each stage

If the provider is running late, you will be notified automatically with the reason and new expected time.

---

### Step 5: Rate Your Provider

After the job is marked as completed, you can tap **"Rate this service"** and give:
- A **star rating** (1–5 stars)
- A **written review**
- **Category-specific ratings** (punctuality, quality, price fairness)

This rating directly affects the provider's **Trust Score** — which helps future customers get better matches.

---

### Step 6: Your Profile

The **👤 Profile** tab shows your account info and gives you the option to sign out.

---

## The Provider Journey

### Step 1: Sign Up as a Provider

On the same login screen, you choose **"I provide services"** instead. After signing up, you are taken to the **Provider Onboarding Wizard** — a 4-step setup process.

---

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile (Onboarding Wizard)

This is a guided, step-by-step process to set up your professional profile:

**Step 1 of 4 — Select Your Services**
Pick the services you offer from checkboxes (AC Repair, Plumbing, Electrical, etc.). You can select multiple.

**Step 2 of 4 — Set Your Location & Experience**
- Enter the area you serve (e.g., "G-13, Islamabad")
- Enter your years of experience
- Write a short bio about yourself

**Step 3 of 4 — Set Your Rates**
For each service you selected, you set your price in PKR. For example:
- AC Repair: PKR 3,000
- Plumbing: PKR 2,000

**Step 4 of 4 — Set Your Availability & Certifications**
- Pick which days of the week you work
- Add any certifications you have (e.g., "HVAC Certified", "Licensed Electrician")

Once you complete all 4 steps and tap **"Complete Setup"**, your profile is saved and you are taken to the Provider Dashboard.

---

### Step 3: Provider Dashboard — Your Command Center

This is the main screen for providers. At the top you see:

**Your greeting and online/offline toggle** — A switch that controls whether you receive new job requests. When you are offline, no new opportunities are sent to you.

**Today's Stats** — Four cards showing real numbers:
- 📋 **Today's Jobs** — How many jobs are scheduled today
- 💰 **Today's Earnings** — How much you have earned today in PKR
- ⭐ **Avg. Rating** — Your average customer rating
- 🏆 **Trust Score** — Your overall reliability score (out of 100)

**Your Services** — Chips showing what services you offer (e.g., "ac repair", "plumbing")

**Incoming Opportunities** — This is where new job requests appear. Each opportunity shows:
- The type of service needed
- The customer's area and distance from you
- The estimated price
- How urgent it is (Today / Upcoming)
- A **match score** (how well this job fits your skills)
- A **countdown timer** showing how long you have to respond

For each opportunity, you have two buttons:
- **✓ Accept Job** — You take the job, and the customer is notified
- **✗ Decline** — You skip it, and it's offered to the next provider

**Quick links** at the bottom take you to your Schedule and Earnings.

---

### Step 4: Job Opportunities Tab (🔔)

This is a dedicated full-screen view of all incoming job opportunities. Each card has:
- Service type and description (e.g., *"AC not cooling, gas refill needed"*)
- Location and distance
- Price estimate
- Match percentage
- A **live countdown timer** that ticks every second — when it reaches zero, the opportunity expires automatically
- The timer turns **red** when you have less than 30 seconds left

You can **pull down to refresh** and get the latest opportunities.

---

### Step 5: My Schedule Tab (📅)

Shows all your accepted jobs for today and upcoming days. Each job card shows:
- The scheduled time (formatted as "Today, 2:30 PM" or "Tomorrow, 10:00 AM")
- The service type
- The customer's name
- The customer's area
- The agreed price
- Current status with color coding

**Updating Job Status:**
You tap **"Update Status →"** to move through the job lifecycle:
- Confirmed → **En Route** (you're heading to the customer)
- En Route → **In Progress** (you've arrived and started working)
- In Progress → **Completed** (job is done)

**Running Late:**
If you're stuck on a job and running behind schedule, you tap **"⏰ Running Late"**. The app asks:
- How much extra time you need (15 min, 30 min, 45 min, 1 hour)
- The reason (job is more complex, waiting for parts, etc.)

Once you submit this, your **next customer is automatically notified** with the delay reason and updated arrival time.

---

### Step 6: Earnings Tab (💰)

A full dashboard of your financial performance:

**Summary Cards:**
- 💰 **This Week's Earnings** — Total PKR earned in the last 7 days
- 🏆 **Trust Score** — Your reliability score

**Rating Display** — Your average star rating with the number of reviews

**Weekly Earnings Chart** — A bar chart showing your earnings for each of the last 7 days, so you can see which days are most profitable

**Trust Score Breakdown** — A visual breakdown showing:
- ⭐ Average Rating (percentage)
- 📝 Total Reviews
- ✅ Completion Rate
- ⚡ Response Time

Each factor has a progress bar so you can see where to improve.

If you're a **new provider** who hasn't completed any jobs yet, you see a friendly message: *"🌟 Complete your first job to start seeing real earnings and ratings here!"*

## What Makes UstaJi Special

1. **Talk, don't tap.** Instead of navigating through forms and dropdowns, you just describe your problem in a chat — in whatever language feels natural. The AI does the rest.

2. **Fair pricing, no haggling.** The price is calculated transparently based on the job complexity, distance, and urgency. Both the customer and the provider see the same number upfront. No surprises.

3. **Smart matching.** The app doesn't just pick a random available worker. It scores every provider on 11 different factors — distance, rating, reliability, skill match, price — and picks the best fit for *your specific job*.

4. **Real-time accountability.** Providers can update their job status live (En Route → In Progress → Done), and if they're running late, the next customer is automatically notified. No more waiting around wondering.

5. **Trust Score system.** Every provider has a Trust Score (0–100) built from their ratings, on-time percentage, completion rate, and response speed. This creates a meritocracy where the best workers naturally rise to the top.

6. **Bilingual by default.** The entire experience — from the AI chat to the category labels to the greetings — works in both English and Urdu, because that's how people in Pakistan actually communicate.
